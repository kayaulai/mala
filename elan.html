ELAN

For texts time-aligned to audio and video, featuring a modular data model and wide import/export support.

Release year: 2004

Paper: https://aclanthology.org/L04-1285/

Code: https://archive.mpi.nl/tla/elan/download

Availability: closed-source

Data types: tags, time-aligned, chunk-spans, overlapping-spans

Import formats: elan-xml, flex-xml, plaintext, csv, json, xml, proprietary

Export formats: elan-xml, flex-xml, plaintext, csv, json, xml, proprietary

Keywords: language-documentation
